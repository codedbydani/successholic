Building Champions On and Off the Court

Welcome to the Strathmore Tennis Team. We are committed to excellence, teamwork, discipline, and growth through tennis. Even as we strive to embody competitive tennis, we also showcase the joy behind playing the sport itself.

About the Team

"Tennis is more than winning matches. It is about discipline, resilience, teamwork, and becoming the best version of yourself."

The Strathmore Tennis Team provides a supportive and competitive environment where players develop their technical skills, build lasting friendships, and proudly represent the university in tournaments and events.

Through regular training sessions, competitions, and team activities, members grow both as athletes and as individuals.

Meet the Captain, Anne Mwangi

Anne Mwangi serves as the captain of the Strathmore Tennis Team, providing leadership, encouragement, and guidance to fellow players both on and off the court.

She has demonstrated dedication, discipline, and sportsmanship throughout her tennis journey while helping to foster a positive and competitive team culture.

Anne has participated in various tournaments and continues to inspire teammates through her commitment to excellence and personal growth.
