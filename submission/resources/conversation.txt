I interviewed Anne Mwangi, the captain of the Strathmore Tennis Team.

Anne explained that the team's purpose is to help students develop their tennis skills, build teamwork, and represent Strathmore University in competitions.

The target audience includes current team members, prospective players, and anyone interested in learning about the team.

She requested that the website highlight the team's activities, achievements, leadership, gallery, and contact information. She preferred a clean, modern, and professional design that reflects discipline, sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence.
